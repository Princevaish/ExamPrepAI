Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated accordingly. There are no penalties or long-term commitments.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with your subscription, simply contact our support team within 7 days of purchase for a full refund.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your account and all generated content remain accessible even after cancellation. You'll be downgraded to the Free plan, but your data is never deleted unless you explicitly request account deletion.
Do you offer student or educational discounts? +
Yes! We offer special discounts for students and educational institutions. Please contact us with your institutional email for more information about our academic pricing.
Can I try Pro features before purchasing? +
Absolutely! You can start with our Free plan to explore the platform. We also offer a 7-day trial for the Pro plan with no credit card required.
